Dear Reader,
The other day, I sat down at my desk
to write a note to a friend. My goal
was to express love and support as she 
goes through a difficult time. 
The amazing thing is that this little
sheet of paper filled with my squiggles
and lines sent that message, but not 
through my words. The message was
delivered through the act of putting ink
to paper ~ though the personal and 
ultimate "hidden" message communicated 
through handwriting. My friends deep 
appreciate of receiving a handwritten
note surprised me.
