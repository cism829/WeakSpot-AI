Gravity The force that attracts toward the centers of the earth is known as gravity.
It's SI unit is w. It is denoted by W or 7. It is a vector quantity. The gravity of the earth depends on mass and radius. It acts towards the centre of the earth.
Free fall - When a body is falling freely only under the effects of gravity without any external resistance it is known as free fall. A body falling in the vacuum is an example of free fall.
